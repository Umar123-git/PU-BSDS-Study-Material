## üî∑ IEEE Style Overview

### üî∏ In-Text Citation:

* Cite sources using **numbers in square brackets** in the **order of appearance**.

  > Example: ‚Äú...as shown in \[1], \[2], and \[3].‚Äù

* Use **et al.** for three or more authors:

  > Example: Smith et al. \[4]

### üî∏ Reference List:

* Title it **"References"**.
* Number each reference **in order of appearance** (not alphabetically).
* Use a **hanging indent** format.
